Welcome to 'What's the Rating?'

Movie ratings are pulled from the Online Movies Database API, an online service to obtain movie information.

Due to rate limitations, users should request a free personal API key by navigating here and following the instructions.

Once a valid key is obtained and activated, you'll be able to search for any movie/TV show on the popup window.

Alternatively, you may right click on Netflix movie titles, press the icon, and get rating notifications directly on your browser (if enabled).
